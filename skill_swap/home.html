SKILL SWAP NETWORK

The skill swap hub is an innovative and transformative solution aimed at revolutionizing the way students learn and support one another. The skill swap hub addresses this gap by providing a tailored platform that intelligently matches students based on their individual proficiency levels and strengths.


The Skill Swap Hub fosters a collaborative learning environment where students can both teach and learn from one another, creating a more dynamic and personalized educational experience. By enabling students to share their unique skills, the platform not only strengthens academic support but also encourages the development of interpersonal and communication skills.
